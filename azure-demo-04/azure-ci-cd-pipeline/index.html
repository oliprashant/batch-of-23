Welcome to The Shire

The Heart of the West

The Shire is a land of rolling green hills, lush farmland, and quiet rivers. It is home to the hobbits, a peaceful folk who love good food, gardens, and simple pleasures. Here, time moves slowly, and life is measured in meals and seasons. The Shire is divided into four main regions: the Northfarthing, Southfarthing, Eastfarthing, and Westfarthing, with Hobbiton and Bywater at its heart.

The Party Tree stands in the center of Hobbiton, a massive oak where Bilbo Baggins held his famous farewell party. The hobbits live in hobbit holes—cozy, circular homes dug into hillsides, with round doors painted in bright colors. It is a land of beauty, peace, and simple joy.

Heroes of the Shire & Beyond

💍 🧝

Frodo Baggins

The Ring-bearer

Frodo inherited the One Ring from his uncle Bilbo and was tasked with destroying it in the fires of Mount Doom. Despite the Ring's corrupting influence, he remained steadfast, carrying the burden to the very end. His journey is a testament to the idea that even the smallest person can change the course of the future.

Samwise Gamgee

The Loyal Gardener

Samwise, Frodo's loyal gardener, is the true hero of the tale. He accompanied Frodo on the quest, providing unwavering support and friendship. Known for his simple wisdom, deep love of the Shire, and steadfast loyalty, Sam embodies humility and courage. After the war, he became Mayor of the Shire.

Bilbo Baggins

The Adventurer

Bilbo is the hobbit who started it all. He embarked on an adventure with Thorin Oakenshield to reclaim the Lonely Mountain, finding the One Ring in Gollum's cave along the way. A lover of maps, riddles, and stories, Bilbo lived to be 131 years old—one of the oldest hobbits in history.

Meriadoc Brandybuck

Master of Buckland
